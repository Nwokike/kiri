## 🔬 Our Mission: Intelligence on the Edge

Kiri is an applied research studio by **Kiri Research Labs**. We bridge the gap between industrial-scale foundation models and the practical constraints of edge deployment. Our focus is on maximizing reasoning fidelity while minimizing compute, memory, and energy footprints.

### Research Pillars
*   **🧩 SLM Engineering**: Developing high-reasoning Small Language Models (0.5B - 3B) optimized for local inference.
*   **🏗️ Cognitive Topologies**: Multi-tier "Manager-Worker" systems for efficient task decomposition and failover.
*   **🗣️ Cultural Bedrocks**: Adaptive tokenization and context-preservation for underrepresented languages.

## 🚀 Key Platform Features

### 1. The Colosseum (Projects)
A curated showcase of compute-optimal tools. Every project is automatically classified and provisioned into the most efficient environment (Lane A, B, or C).  
👉 [See Traffic Controller Docs](docs/TRAFFIC_CONTROLLER.md)

### 2. Kiri Studio (v1.2 Stable)
A zero-server, client-side IDE for "Vibe Coding." Powered by **Pyodide** (WASM), it enables full Python execution directly in the browser with strict COOP/COEP isolation.  
👉 [Explore Kiri Studio Docs](docs/KIRI_STUDIO.md)

### 3. Intelligence Orchestration
A multi-tier AI failover system utilizing 15+ models across providers (Moonshot, Llama 4, Gemini 3) to ensure 100% uptime for complex reasoning tasks.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

Kiri is engineered for the **"Zero-Server"** era:
*   **Backend**: Django 6.0 + HTMX 2.0 (Zero-JS overhead philosophy).
*   **Aesthetics**: Tailwind CSS v4 (Modern, fast, and utility-first).
*   **Runtime**: WASM / Pyodide for client-side execution.
*   **Observability**: Integrated Error Tracking and Performance Monitoring.
