About Kiri

The operating system for modern research.

Our Mission

Kiri exists to democratize access to research tools and knowledge. We believe that great ideas should be shared freely, and that collaboration accelerates innovation. Our platform brings together researchers, developers, and enthusiasts from around the world to share their work, learn from each other, and build the future together.

What We Offer

Library

A curated collection of research papers, tutorials, and technical write-ups from our community.

Projects

Open-source projects and tools showcased by developers from the Kiri community.

Kiri Research Labs

Kiri is developed and maintained by Kiri Research Labs, a company dedicated to building tools that empower researchers and developers worldwide. Founded with the vision of making research more accessible, we continue to grow and evolve with input from our community.
