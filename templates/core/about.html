Who We Are

Kiri Research Labs is an applied research entity and product studio focused on TinyML, Hierarchical Architectures, and Cognitive Infrastructure. We bridge the gap between massive foundation models and the practical reality of deployment in resource-constrained environments.

We reject the premise that intelligence requires massive scale. Instead, we engineer compute-optimal systems and adaptive tokenizers designed to deliver high-reasoning performance on edge hardware.

Core Focus

  • TinyML & SLMs (0.5B - 3B Models)
  • Hierarchical "Manager-Worker" Topologies
  • Acoustic & Linguistic Native Nuances (Naija Voice)
  • Intelligence Scalable to Zero-Compute
